project description

The vision of Vestibule is to build an Augmented Reality platform where people can sell and buy properties, and to incorporate AR technology for the convenience of people and provide an interactive platform to architects and realtors to showcase their portfolios. This app would help people who are unable to leave their homes due to medical reasons (COVID) or miscellaneous reasons and be able to explore a 3D model of homes, schools, or recreational spaces from the comfort of being indoors.
