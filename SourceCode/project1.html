project description
The idea behind our project is being able to show architectural projects without the need of leaving your home. In a time of covid, not being able to leave your home can be an issue due to quarantines or taking care of family members who are sick. Wanting to buy a home from the comfort of being indoors can be now done with Vestibule. With Vestibule you will be able to see 3d models of structures such as residential homes, schools, and recreational spaces from a single picture.
